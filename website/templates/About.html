Fake News Classifier

This system has been proposed keeping in mind the difficulties that people face during registering complaint at any police station.

First of all, the entire manual process is time consuming as the complainant has to physically go to the police station numerous times. The same also consumes a whole lot of money and energy. Other disadvantageous factors include, Fear of getting harmed from people against whom FIR is filed, Lodging FIR against highly reputed person is sometimes difficult task. By allowing citizens to lodge their complaints directly, this system circumvents police officers who are often reluctant to register FIRs, particularly in kidnapping and ransom cases. Potentially, this could be an effective tool in combating the endemic corruption and pressure at the thana level.
